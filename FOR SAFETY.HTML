The Eye Behind

I am Prasad — founder and lead artist at Retro Captures. For twelve years I have pursued a singular obsession: capturing the invisible. The breath before the first kiss. The light that falls exactly once.

My work is rooted in classic film processing and the vocabulary of cinema. I treat every session as a director treats a scene — with intention, patience, and an unyielding respect for the authentic moment.

Clients across India, Europe, and the Middle East trust me with their most irreplaceable days. I do not take that lightly.
